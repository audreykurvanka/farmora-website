About Us

A Trusted Partner for Indonesian Coconut Products

FARMORA is a company engaged in the sourcing, processing, and distribution of Indonesian coconut products for local and international markets. We work closely with selected coconut farmers and suppliers to deliver consistent, high-quality products through responsible sourcing practices.

Our main products include whole coconuts, copra, and coconut shell charcoal. With a strong supply network and strict quality control, FARMORA is committed to becoming a trusted partner for trading, manufacturing, and industrial needs.

Vision

To become a trusted and globally competitive supplier of Indonesian coconut products.

Mission

To provide high-quality coconut products with sustainable supply.

To support the welfare of local farmers and business partners.

To maintain product standards, timely delivery, and customer satisfaction.

To develop coconut-based products professionally and responsibly.

Our Products

Three Core Products We Supply

Whole Coconuts

Fresh whole coconuts sourced from selected Indonesian plantations, available in various sizes and maturity levels based on customer requirements.

For: fresh consumption, food & beverage production, coconut milk, coconut oil processing.

Copra

Copra made from mature coconut meat and dried under controlled conditions to maintain product quality and moisture content.

For: coconut oil production, animal feed, oleochemical products, related industries.

Coconut Shell Charcoal
